Comedy Booker

He has been producing shows their since 1993 where he has booked literally hundreds of shows with hundreds of comics. Headliners who have appeared on his shows include Jon Stewart, Chris Rock, Lewis Black, Ray Romano, Joy Behar, Dave Attel and many others. A frequent host has been Eddie Brill(who is David Lettermans Segment producer for comedians)Another favorite host is Susie Essman (Curb your enthusiasm)These shows are an industry hangout because they are a rite of passage for every young comic. In addition to his good relationships to many comics and people in the comedy industry,he also has experience with booking corporate CEOS. He is the co-producer of the show funny Business which has featured Carl Icahn, Barbra Corchan, Donny Duestch, and many other titans of Business.
